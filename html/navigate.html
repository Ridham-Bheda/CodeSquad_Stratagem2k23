Navigation Instructions

To navigate through each webpage on this site, follow these instructions:

Sign In Page:

Enter your email and password, then click the "Sign In" button.

Sign Up Page:

Fill out the form with your information, then click the "Sign Up" button.

Patient Registration Dashboard:

After signing in, you will be redirected to the patient registration dashboard. Here, you can fill out your personal and medical information, as well as view your appointments and medical records.

Admin Registration Dashboard:

If you are an admin, you can access the admin registration dashboard by clicking the "Admin Dashboard" link in the top menu. Here, you can manage patient and hospital information, as well as view reports and statistics.
