Navigation Instructions

To navigate through each webpage on this site, follow these instructions:

Sign In Page:

Enter your email and password, then click the "Sign In" button.

Sign Up Page:

Enter your email and password, then select from drop down menu either 'Patient' or 'Hospital'.

Fill the form by entering your basic information, then click on "Sign Up" button.

Select your Hospital

After signing in, you will be redirected to the patient home page. Here, you can select hospital and click on register.

Patient Dashboard:

On patient's dashboard, you can see your details and hospitals list that you are registered in

Admin Dashboard:

On admin's dashboard, you can see hospital name and registered patient names
